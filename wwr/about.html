South Team Rafting Company

About our company

At South Team Rafting Company, our mission is to provide exhilarating and safe whitewater rafting experiences that connect our customers with the thrill of nature. We are committed to fostering a spirit of adventure and exploration, ensuring that every guest leaves with unforgettable memories and a deepened appreciation for the great outdoors.

History

Founded in 2010 by a group of passionate river guides, South Team Rafting Team started with a single vision: to share the excitement and beauty of whitewater rafting with people from all walks of life. What began as a small outfit with just a few rafts and a deep love for the rapids has grown into one of the region's most respected rafting companies.

Today, South Team Rafting Team is proud to have introduced thousands of adventurers to the thrills of the rapids, all while maintaining an impeccable safety record and a commitment to conservation. The company looks forward to many more years of guiding explorers through the exhilarating waterways that have defined its journey
